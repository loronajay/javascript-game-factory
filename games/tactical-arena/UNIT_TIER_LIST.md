# Tactical Arena — Unit Tier List

My ranking of the 30 draftable units, S (best) down to D. This is the **clean list** —
reasoning for every placement lives in `UNIT_TIER_LIST_REASONING.md`.

Context: standard 4-unit custom squads, 1v1 (hot-seat or online blind-pick/draft),
13×13 or 15×15 board, RAGE auto-triggers at ≤5 HP. Ranked by overall power and
flexibility across the draft, not by ceiling in one perfect comp.

---

## S — format-defining, draft blind
- **Mystic** — unconditional +1 team DEF, cheap board-wide healing, full cleanse, absurd rage
- **Nemesis** — best offensive enabler: +1 team magic damage *and* −1 MP per ART, team-wide
- **Necromancer** — −1 team magic taken + −1 enemy DEF aura + free extra bodies (Ghouls)
- **Gargoyle** — immovable, fully status-immune, pierce-AoE, and the best rage in the roster

## A — strong and flexible
- **Paladin** — lifelink aura + white-tile DEF + full status immunity + free bonus-action chip
- **Clod** — highest DEF; negates *all* physical while defending; phalanx anchor
- **Monk** — permanent move-and-ART, ignore-body movement, impact-stun kick, and bodyguard reaction
- **Juggernaut** — board-wide heal lockout in rage (un-rageable), efficient true-AoE finisher
- **Magician** — the clean armor solvent; self-refueling; Nuke rage wipes clusters
- **Fat Wizard** — durable self-sufficient caster/healer; Lazy Cast rage is a win condition
- **Virus** — the status multiplier; turns any single debuff into board-wide lockdown (S-ceiling)
- **Father Time** — unavoidable attrition aura that refuels itself, plus a full-HP revive

## B — solid role-players and build-arounds
- **Angel** — anti-armor magic support, full status immunity, elite bonus-action rage
- **Witch Doctor** — rules-editor stances (global ×2 status, the only real MP battery)
- **Mother Nature** — weather rules-editor, 100 MP, roll-free control (A-ceiling in a built squad)
- **Fat Cleric** — the most durable healer; cleanse + revive; survives her own rage
- **Sniper** — longest range, pierces walls *and* bodies; the anti-turtle pick
- **Riot Cop** — sturdy peeler; blanks non-critical magic while defending; cooldown-style economy
- **Little Brother** — crit-splash/fire artillery; casters refuel him hard
- **Fat Knight** — anti-crit brick; Trample rage line-damage
- **Treant** — −1 team magic taken + positional DEF + invulnerable heal-pulse rage
- **Blacksword** — dark-tile duelist with a real self-chain combo and a board-wipe ultimate

## C — situational / conditional
- **Fat Bowman** — strong turret if she can plant; punished for any repositioning
- **Summoner** — high tempo ceiling, but high-variance and a fragile, presence-less body
- **Big Brother** — unique anti-heal/true-damage tech; near-dead into non-healing comps
- **Swordsman** — reliable baseline utility bruiser, but outclassed by the rebuild melee
- **Ronin** — high personal threat, but his anti-team passive fights a 4-unit squad
- **Miner** — safe, uncontested early setup; a working range-5 ranger throttled by his own ammo economy

## D — hardest to justify / worst floor
- **King** — a comeback engine with an S-ceiling, but a genuine liability floor (can't win alone)
- **Archer** — fragile and paradoxical; outclassed by every rebuild ranger

---

*Not ranked: **Ghoul** — summon-only (raised by the Necromancer), never drafted.*
