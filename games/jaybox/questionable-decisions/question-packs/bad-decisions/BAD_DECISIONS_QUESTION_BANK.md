# Questionable Decisions — Bad Decisions Question Bank

**Status:** Canon v1  
**Question count:** 40  
**Scoring:** 100, 200, 300, or 400 points  
**Answer key:** Stored separately to avoid spoiling the category for players.

## Category Identity

**Bad Decisions** is recognition-based trivia about famous mistakes, ignored warnings, failed products, disastrous plans, and poor choices from movies, television, stories, history, sports, games, and popular culture.

The mistake should already be entertaining. Questions should not rely on invented comedy scenarios, moral lessons, specialist education, or subjective judgment.

### Difficulty Standard

- **100 points:** Broad recognition. Most mixed-age groups should have a strong chance.
- **200 points:** Familiar subject, but requires a specific piece of recall.
- **300 points:** Less common detail from a recognizable subject. Challenging, but not academic or specialist trivia.
- **400 points:** Specific failure, name, or consequence from a recognizable disaster. Hard, but still answerable from cultural memory.
