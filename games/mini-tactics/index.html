How to play

  • Each player activates every living piece once, then the squad turn changes.
  • Warrior moves 3 orthogonal tiles. Tank, ranger, and medic move 2.
  • Attack range includes diagonals with no penalty: warrior/tank 1, medic 3, ranger 4.
  • Ranger shots are blocked by any piece between ranger and target.
  • Attack/heal roll: 1 misses, 6 crits.
  • Critical attacks add 1 damage. Critical heals restore 4 instead of 3.
  • Warrior deals 3, but only 2 to tanks. Tank deals 2. Ranger deals 2, or 3 to another ranger. Medic deals 1.
  • Defending reduces each incoming hit by 1, including to zero, and expires when that piece is next selected to act.
  • Move must be paired with attack, heal, or defend. Attack/heal may happen before or after movement.
  • Last squad standing wins.
