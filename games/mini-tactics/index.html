    How to Play

    • Each player activates every living piece once, then the squad turn changes.
    • A piece may move then act, or act then move — but a move alone is illegal. Every activation must end in an attack, heal, or defend.
    • Movement is orthogonal: warrior & tank 3 tiles, ranger/medic 2. Pieces block movement.
    • Attack range includes diagonals with no penalty: warrior/tank 1, medic 3, ranger 4.
    • Ranger shots are blocked by any piece between ranger and target — including your own.
    • Attack/heal roll: 1 misses, 6 crits. Crit attacks add 1 damage; crit heals restore 4 instead of 3.
    • Defending reduces each incoming hit by 1 (down to zero) and expires when that piece is next selected to act.
    • Cancel Move undoes an uncommitted move before the piece attacks, heals, or defends.
    • Last squad standing wins.

    Medic Support
    10HP 2Move 3Range 1Damage

    Heals 3 HP (4 on a crit) to a teammate or itself within range 3. Its attack is just a weak 1-damage poke — heal, don't trade.

    Every unit has 10 HP. On an attack roll, 1 misses and 6 crits (+1 damage; a Medic crit heals 4). Defend cuts each incoming hit by 1 (to zero) until that unit next acts.

    • Focus fire beats heals. A medic heals about one Warrior's worth per turn. Pile 2+ attackers on one target so the heal can't keep up — spread chip damage just gets topped off.
    • Burst before they heal. Squads act as a block, so your whole turn lands before the enemy medic responds. Damage that kills this turn can't be undone. Set up the kill; don't chip.
    • Know the matchups. Warriors hit Tanks for only 2 — send them at Rangers and Medics instead. Your Ranger does +1 to the enemy Ranger: use it as a counter-sniper.
    • Kill or pin the Medic. A medic only one attacker can reach is nearly unkillable (it out-heals a single attacker). Get two threats on it, or pressure two targets so its one heal can't cover both.
    • Mind line of sight. Ranger shots stop on any body, including your own. Keep your lanes clear, and park units in the enemy Ranger's lanes to block it.
    • Defend the chip, not the crit. Defend only removes 1 — huge against Tank/Ranger/Medic pokes, minor against a Warrior. Time it to cover the enemy's whole turn.
    • Threat = move + reach. A Warrior threatens 4 tiles away. Keep your Ranger and Medic outside every enemy's move-plus-reach box, not just their current tile.
    • Scout with Cancel Move. Move a piece to see its highlights and line of sight, then Cancel if the angle is wrong — free board reading before you commit.
